Your Questions Answered

Everything you need to know about our program

What age group is this program for?

This microcourse is specially designed for children in Grades 3-5 (ages 8-11). The activities are tailored to build confidence and communication skills appropriate for this developmental stage.

Are sessions conducted online?

Yes, all sessions are conducted live online through our secure platform. We use interactive tools, games, and breakout rooms to keep children engaged. You'll need a device with camera and microphone capabilities.

How can I book a spot?

You can book directly using the "Enroll Now" button or contact our support team at hello@scoreazy.com. We recommend booking at least 3 days in advance as batches fill up quickly.

What's the class duration and schedule?

The program runs for 5 consecutive days with 60-minute sessions each day. We offer multiple time slots (morning and evening) to accommodate different schedules. You'll select your preferred time during enrollment.

Do you offer any guarantees?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If your child doesn't show noticeable improvement in confidence after completing the program, we'll provide a full refund or free additional coaching sessions.
